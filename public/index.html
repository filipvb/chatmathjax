Chat MathJax - MathJax included to render formulas

Surround (wrap) your TeX/LaTeX with \( and \) for inline math and with \[ and \] for display math rendering ($...$ for inline; $$....$$ for display math would also work).

If you want just to show the command text do not wrap it. If you want to see the TeX/LaTeX for a rendered formula right-click on it.
